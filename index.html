Rainy day

Rainy Days shows you weather radar doppler observations from multiple sources on a fully interactive map.

NOTE: Currently there's an outage at weather.gov which means that data may not be fully up to date!

You can zoom in, pan and search for locations. Rainy Days uses multiple sources of data, not one map for the whole world. This gives you the most recent data for your location. Therefore, Rainy Days works best when zooming in to your local area.
